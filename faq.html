Frequently Asked Question

CAN I REPLACE A PLANT I BOUGHT IN GROWER’S POT?

If your plant dies within first 3 days, we will replace it free of charge. Provide pictures for this replacement type. After 3 days period, we offer 15% discount on a new plant purchase + delivery fee + sales tax (if in New York). Replacement request form is at the bottom of our Replacement Policy. Please include your order number.

WHAT IS YOUR CANCELATION/REFUND POLICY?

If order is canceled by a customer prior a delivery date, a customer will get a full refund. If order is canceled on a day of delivery, a customer will get a full refund minus re-stocking fee of $40.00 per plant. All other order cancellations are non refundable.

WHICH INDOOR PLANTS ARE THE EASIEST TO CARE FOR?

Pothos, Aglaonema, Sansevieria, Dracaena Lisa, Spider, Corn, Iron, ZZ plants, Bird’s Nest fern, Cacti, Succulents.

I BOUGHT A PLANT IN GROWER’S POT. WHEN SHOULD I REPOT MY PLANT AND WHAT KIND OF POT TO LOOK FOR?

It is best to repot your plant as it is not supposed to live in its growing pot. If you do not see the roots poking out of drainage holes, you can wait for a few months and take your time to find a perfect pot for your green baby. Otherwise, repot it rather sooner than later. Choose a pot 1-3 inches larger than the original container. Pots with drainage are always the best choice but if you found an awesome cache pot that you must have, make sure to add a good layer of rocks at the bottom of a pot to provide a drainage for your plant.

DO YOU SHIP TO ANY COUNTRY OUTSIDE OF MALAYSIA?

At the moment we do not ship overseas. We plan to do so for our range of pots, plant stand and tools soon.
