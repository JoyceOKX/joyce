At GO GREEN, tourist are given the oppurtunity to participate in multiple choices of activity such as fruit tasting and planting lessons. We also have a cafe for toursits to visit after a tiring day at the garden. Every year, millions of trees are cut down and has affected wildlife, ecosystems and weather patterns. To deal with this issue, GO GREEN was founded back in 2008 to raise funds so that the money collected can be used to plant more trees. It was then upgraded to a better user-friendly environemt to encourage people to love mother nature for a better future.
