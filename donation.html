Donation for Plants

As of the year 2016, GO GREEN has managed to raise thousands of ringgit through extra incomes and profits to plant more plants and trees for a better environment.
In the year 2017, the company decided to introduce to a donation page. The goal is to recieve donations and use the money to organise campaigns that encourages people to plant trees. Not only that, money collected will also be used to help for the poor in terms of food and clothing.
